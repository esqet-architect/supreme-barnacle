## Critical Caveats

- ESQET is a **speculative EFT framework**, not a UV-complete theory
- RG equations are **truncation-dependent**; not scheme-independent invariants
- The golden ratio φ serves as a **heuristic organizing motif**, not a derived constant
- The spectral tilt n_T ≈ 4.90 is a **transient effective exponent**, not an asymptotic prediction
- Observational estimates are **order-of-magnitude illustrations**
